About Engiitex

Energy cost for environmental sustainability, carbon footprint, and advancements in materials, control systems, and enabling software/hardware continue to drive innovation in vehicle systems and transportation. This project has historically focused on the refuelable batteries for electric vehicles. However, over the years of the IPRO Program, IPRO teams have worked on ethanol vehicles (including aircraft), fuel cell vehicles (land and submerged), vehicles as storage elements in a future renewable energy dominated grid, etc. Due to the high demand from students, the IPRO this semester is going beyond just the refuelable electric vehicle and focusing on a multi-team project organized in four or more discrete multidisciplinary teams that would each tackle an innovative vehicle system. The projects include: zinc batteries, six stroke, hybrid battery, liquid cooling systems, hover board/car, and the business team. These six teams all fall under the Business brand called Engiitex (engiitex.github.io).

Engiitex is a newly formed company that consists of four different departments: Research and development (R&D), Finance, Marketing, and Production. The mission of Engiitex is to positively contribute to the Transportation industry through our research in Liquid cooling systems, Hybrid batteries, and Zinc batteries technology as well as introducing innovative design for the Six Stoke Engine and Hover car technologies. The Business/Management team will oversee the finances, marketing, and production branches of the company.
