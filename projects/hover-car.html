The Hover Car Project

During this semester we will try to achieve the following goals:

  • Gather data about the heat, force produced, power needed
  • Create an optimized design for our custom build final prototype
  • Make our prototype lift about 1000 pounds (1/4 of an average carâ€™s weight)
  • Integrate an internal cooling system in design
  • Use a circuit that would be able to reuse a lot of the energy used in the process so that the battery would only need to supply the losses in the system.
  • Come up with ideas to integrate an accelerating/breaking system

The teams coming after us will have everything we have work on for this semester however, the challenges for them would be as follows:

  • Optimization of the cooling system
  • Optimizing the circuit that reuses the power put in
  • Searching for different materials that our product as well as the floor can be made out of
  • Creating the accelerating/breaking system
  • Specify where our application could be useful and estimate production prices
