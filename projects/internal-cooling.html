Internal Battery Cooling

Pitch

Our team is involved in researching the feasibility of cooling the battery of an electric vehicle through the use of phase changing materials. As you may know, cooling is a problem for current electric vehicle batteries, and therefore, a more moderated charging regime, which can often be time-consuming and inefficient. So, we’re developing a technology that would overcome this limitation. This would allow for a fast charge to be possible without the risk of damaging the equipment.

Currently, we’re in the material research phase, and we’re looking to move to the battery design and prototyping phase soon. If successful, we’d like to contract with a national automobile company that would adopt our technology into their cars.
