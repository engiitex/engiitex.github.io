The Phase Change Material Battery Cooling Project

Mission

To promote environmentally friendly transportation tool, the demand for lithium-ion batteries will surge in the following decades with the EV market growing.

Previous Work

According to previous papers and programs, although lithium-ion battery owns a major endorsement of its consistent and reliable performance in an automotive environment, the heat generated by battery itself can impact the battery security and discharging efficiency, which leads our attention to battery thermal management system. As for the conventional electrical vehicles, they utilize air-cooling as the single method to dissipate battery heat while charging, which is heavily dependent on battery electricity and intensifies the burden of battery at the same time.

Current Research and Work

Consequently, our researches focus on regulating battery temperature in a safe and energy-efficient way based on hybrid method of phase change materials (PCM) and natural air-cooling. When PCM melts at a certain temperature, it can stores large amounts of energy. So the PCM battery pack will be utilized in producing lithium-ion battery pack which can absorb heat while charging or discharging. Natural air-cooling can also dissipate the heat from PCM battery pack while the vehicle is moving. In the meanwhile, the layout of air flue will be optimized for the best performance of transferring heat. In addition, PCM battery packs can utilize waste heat for battery warm during the winter period. In contrast, this hybrid battery thermal management system will provide better property than conventional scheme, which will guarantee the vehicle driving security.
